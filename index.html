How it works

FROM WARM-UP TO
INSIGHTS IN MINUTES

Booah fits into your existing training workflow. No disruption to sessions, just data.

01
Equip your squad

Players attach lightweight sEMG sensors to targeted muscle groups before the session begins. Full squad setup takes under five minutes.

02
Train as normal

Sensors stream real-time surface EMG data throughout the entire session. Muscle activation amplitude, fatigue indicators, and bilateral load distribution are captured continuously at the individual level.

03
Act on the insights

The Booah platform surfaces clear, actionable insights - flagged asymmetries, high-risk athletes, and team-level load trends - ready in your coaching dashboard by end of session.
